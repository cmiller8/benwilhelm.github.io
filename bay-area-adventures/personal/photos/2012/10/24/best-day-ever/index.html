Best. Day. Ever.

Oct 24, 2012

Posted in - bay area adventures - personal - photos

Wow.  It’s taken me a couple of days to fully process the three days full of love and joy that were our wedding, but now Jen and I want to say a huge THANK YOU to all of the people that came out to California or just sent their well-wishes and made this weekend the happiest, most perfect that we could have possibly imagined.

Getting married is awesome, y’all.

It’s been 5 months (to the day) since I last posted to my blog, because that’s about when all of the crazy started.  We had just kicked the wedding planning from “casual” to “serious” mode when Jen got hired back in Chicago, and we discovered were were going to be moving. Quickly. To make a long story short, within six weeks of Jen getting the job, we had packed up and moved back to Chicago.  Six weeks after that, Jen ran the Chicago Marathon, and two weeks later we were back in California getting married. The last four months have been a bit nuts, but this weekend we remembered what it was all for.
